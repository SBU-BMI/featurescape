FeatureScape Interactive Tool to Allow Feature Exploration Across Multiple Dimensions

Morphology features extracted from image analysis of histology whole slide images for 522 Lung Adenocarcinoma Patients of The Cancer Genome Atlas. Genomic data compiled with MSKCC cBioPortal tool, demographic and clinical data from TCGA repository. This tool is figured in the report Saltz J, JS Almeida, Yi Gao, A Sharma, E Bremer, T DiPrima, M Saltz, J Kalpathy-Cramer, T Kurc (2017). Towards Generation, Management, and Exploration of Combined Radiomics and Pathomics Datasets for Cancer Research. AMIA Joint Summits on Translational Science Proceedings.
